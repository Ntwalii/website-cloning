What is HomeAway?

FeWo-direkt is your competent contact for holidays in a holiday home or holiday apartment . As part of Vrbo, one of the leading online vacation home rental marketplaces, Vrbo offers you more than two million unique properties, including fincas, bungalows, apartments, cabins and even villas . Secure payments, 24/7 support and the Book with Confidence Guarantee make bookings a breeze. Using the user-friendly website and numerous practical filters, you can easily find your dream holiday apartment for your budget. You're sure to have an unforgettable vacation that's completely stress-free, from the search and booking to your stay and departure.

Renting a holiday apartment or holiday home: These are the advantages

Families and groups in particular appreciate the advantages of holiday accommodation: more space, more privacy, more amenities. You have more flexibility and freedom in your own four walls than in conventional hotels and can plan your vacation freely - regardless of whether you want to go on a city trip or relax with your dog in the middle of picturesque nature. Are you looking for a cozy holiday cabin in the mountains with a washing machine and whirlpool ? A beach house on the water or a large finca in the south with a pool and fully equipped kitchen? No problem, because holiday accommodation is available in all sizes and with every equipment you want for your next family holiday , adventure trip with friends or a romantic weekend.
